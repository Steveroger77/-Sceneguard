# 🛡️ SceneGuard

**The AI-Powered "Spot the Difference" for Your Physical Space.**

SceneGuard is an intelligent monitoring system that helps you keep track of your belongings. By using advanced AI, it "remembers" how a space should look and alerts you instantly if something moves, goes missing, or if a new object appears.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **📸 One-Tap Reference:** Snap a "Master Photo" of your desk, room, or storefront to set a baseline.
- **👁️ Real-Time Monitoring:** The app watches your space through your camera and detects changes on the fly.
- **🤖 Smart Detection:**
  - **Present (Green):** Everything is exactly where it should be.
  - **Missing (Red):** Alert! Something that was there before is now gone.
  - **New (Blue):** A new object has been detected in your space.
- **📦 Intelligent Highlighting:** Automatic colorful boxes (Bounding Boxes) appear around objects to show exactly what the AI is seeing.
- **🔒 Privacy First:** All your saved scenes are stored locally in your browser—not on a mystery server.

---

## 🛠️ How It Works (Simple Steps)

1. **Setup a Scene:** Go to "Create Reference" and take a clear photo of your space (e.g., your desk with your laptop and keys).
2. **Start Monitoring:** Switch to "Monitor" mode.
3. **AI Comparison:** The app uses **Google Gemini AI** and **TensorFlow** to compare your live camera feed against your saved photo.
4. **Get Alerts:** If you pick up your keys, the AI will highlight that spot in red and tell you they are "Missing."

## 🖥️ Getting Started

1. Open the app and allow **Camera Access**.
2. Select your preferred camera from the dropdown menu.
3. Create your first reference and let the AI guard your scene!
